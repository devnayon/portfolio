How to describe yourself?

1 - Start with a concise opening line that tells readers who you are. 2 - Your online portfolio is your space, so there's no need to be overly formal. Write the way you naturally speak. 3 - Decide which professional experiences to include. 4 - Mention your educational background, relevant projects, and any notable achievements. Keep it descriptive but not overly lengthy. 5 - Add a few personal details to make your bio relatable. Share a hobby, a fun fact, or something that reflects your interests beyond your profession. 6 - Humanize your portfolio by including a professional headshot.

Remember, your portfolio is a reflection of both your work and your personality. Be authentic, approachable, and confident.
